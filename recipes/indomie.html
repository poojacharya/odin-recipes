Indomie

Description

Indomie noodles are a quick and easy comfort food loved by many. This super simple version uses just two key ingredients—instant noodles and a single egg—to elevate the flavor and texture. The egg adds a rich, creamy texture to the broth, making the meal feel a little more satisfying. Perfect for busy days or when you're craving a fast and tasty meal with minimal effort.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Indomie Instant Noodles (any flavor)
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pot. Add the noodles and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  2. Push the noodles to one side and crack the egg into the pot. Let it poach for 1–2 minutes (or stir for scrambled egg).
  3. Drain most of the water, add the Indomie seasoning and oil from the packet, mix well, and serve hot.
    4. Optional extras: sliced green onions, chili flakes, or a dash of soy sauce.
